Pizza Soup

Description

Pizza Soup is a delicious soup that is easy to make and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of pizza
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of cheese
  • 1 cup of bread
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of salt

Steps

  1. Boil the water and add the pizza, cheese, bread, sugar, and salt
  2. Cook the pizza until it is done
