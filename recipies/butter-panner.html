Butter Panner

Description

Butter Panner is a delicious dish that is easy to make and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of panner
  • 1 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of cream
  • 1 cup of cheese
  • 1 cup of bread
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of salt

Steps

  1. Boil the panner in water
  2. Add the butter, milk, cream, cheese, bread, sugar, and salt to the panner
  3. Cook the panner until it is done
